I’m a full-stack software engineer based in Grapevine, TX, focused on performance, scalability, and clean architecture. At Paycom I architect microservices and search pipelines across millions of candidates, and ship AI-powered hiring features that enterprise teams rely on every day — including interview scheduling automation and a natural language query interface for the iWant product.

I hold an MS in Computer Science from UT Arlington (GPA 3.9/4.0) and a BE in Computer Engineering from Don Bosco Institute of Technology, Mumbai. I’m also IEEE-published — my NLP/ML pipeline was presented at the IEEE Global Conference for Advancement in Technology (GCAT 2022). My work spans enterprise SaaS, mobile apps, and NGO non-profits — always with measurable impact: 40% more event registrations, 20% organizational growth, a 97% reduction in API latency.

Beyond engineering, I founded DBIT SevaTech — a student-run non-profit delivering free tech services to NGOs — and served as Technical Head of ACM-DBIT, one of Mumbai’s largest student chapters.
